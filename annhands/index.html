Hi, I'm Ann, a massage therapist living in Cohasset, Ma.

 

I began practicing massage in 1988 after going thru 20 months of physiotherapy rehabilitation due to a car accident in 1985. Fortunately for me, I had a Norwegian therapist that valued "hands on" therapy. Any person that can help you get out of pain & get your life back is extremely influential in the next path you choose, so I began my "career student" role as a therapist. I call my technique "Integrative Massage" because I blend several years of studying Eastern & Western philosophies to create a session designed to meet the individual clients needs at treatment time. By combining hot stones, use of alternating rhythmic pressures, use of organic & chemical free products, I believe I offer a multidimensional healing experience.

 

Some of my interests are

  • Mixing new and invigorating aroma oils
  • Continued studies of the healing arts
  • Shiatsu and Asian modalities
  • Fitness and Wellbeing
  • My family and my community
